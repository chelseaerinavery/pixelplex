We welcome gamers of all kinds!

What is PixelPlex?

This is locally operated community arcade and store for all things video games. Our knowledgeable staff are passionate about gaming and are here to help you find the perfect game, console, or accessory that suits your interests and preferences. We offer a diverse selection of games, ranging from classic titles to the latest releases, and we strive to make our inventory accessible to everyone. We also feature a large area for demoing games and hosting events that is able to house 50+ gamers and/or spectators!

What's so special about PixelPlex?

Oh, so much. But we are super proud of our mission for inclusivity. We want to smash the video gaming stereotypes. Whether you're a hardcore gamer or a casual player, we invite you to join our community and experience the joy of gaming in a supportive and inclusive atmosphere.

Aren't video games like, bad for you?

Everything in balance is our motto. We don't support addiction in any form, but we believe that with balance in mind, video gaming can lead to improved cognitive skills (in children and adults), stress relief, more socialization (with friends live, online, or at one of our amazing live gaming events!), and can be an incredible educational tool!

"Video games are a platform for players to become their best selves. When you play a game, you're not just challenging yourself, you're challenging the limits of what's possible."

- Gabe Newell, co-founder of Valve Corporation.
